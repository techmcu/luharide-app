Who we are

LuhaRide

LuhaRide is an Uttarakhand-focused taxi platform built to make bookings simple, trusted, and union-friendly.

TECHMCU

The platform is operated by TECHMCU with a strong focus on local union networks and digital growth.

How this helps

For passengers

Find rides by route and date, book seats, and get live trip updates.

For drivers

Post rides, manage trips, and get direct demand through online bookings.

For union admins

Improve district-wise visibility, control ride schedules, and strengthen online presence.
