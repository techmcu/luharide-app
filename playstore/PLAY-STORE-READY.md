## 4. STORE LISTING — ENGLISH

### App Name (30 chars max)

```
LuhaRide
```

### Short Description (80 chars max)

### Tags (Play Console → Store listing → Tags)
```
Uttarakhand, taxi booking, shared taxi, cab booking, hill taxi, seat booking, ride sharing, union driver
```

### Full Description (4000 chars max)
```
LuhaRide is Uttarakhand's own taxi booking platform — connecting passengers with registered taxi union drivers for safe, affordable, and transparent rides across the hills.

**Why LuhaRide?**

- Book individual seats or full vehicles — no need to wait at taxi stands
- Choose from verified union drivers with ratings and reviews
- Real-time trip tracking so your family knows you're safe
- Transparent fares — see the price before you book
- Works across Uttarakhand — Dehradun, Haridwar, Rishikesh, Mussoorie, Nainital, Almora, and more

**For Passengers:**
- Search rides by route, date, and time
- View driver profiles, ratings, and vehicle details
- Book seats instantly — get driver contact for coordination
- Rate and review your ride experience
- File complaints directly through the app

**For Drivers & Taxi Unions:**
- Create and manage trips with flexible scheduling
- Union admins can manage their fleet and drivers
- Build your reputation through passenger reviews
- Digital poster generation for route advertising
- Real-time notifications for new bookings

**Safety First:**
- All drivers are KYC-verified (Aadhaar, Driving Licence, Vehicle RC)
- Document watermarking prevents misuse
- Rating system maintains quality
- In-app complaint system for quick resolution

**Built for Uttarakhand:**
LuhaRide understands pahadi travel. Whether you're commuting daily between Dehradun and Mussoorie, or planning a trip to Chopta, we connect you with local drivers who know every curve of the road.

Download LuhaRide — your trusted travel companion in Uttarakhand!
```
