## KYC & verification documents — how long we keep them

- **Purpose:** Driver and union **identity / vehicle documents** are collected only to **verify** accounts, meet **legal and safety** expectations, and reduce **fraud**.
- **Retention:** We keep these files **only as long as needed** for active verification, ongoing compliance, and **dispute prevention** while your **account exists**.
- **After account closure:** We **delete or anonymise** verification documents within a **reasonable period**, except where **applicable law** requires us to keep copies longer.
- **Security:** Files are stored on **access-controlled servers** with **industry-standard safeguards**. We **do not sell** your document images or personal data to third parties for marketing.

We treat rider and driver data with **care proportional to a transport marketplace**: limited collection, secured storage, and **clear channels** (email above) if something looks wrong.
