Privacy, KYC retention & grievance

LuhaRide is operated under TECHMCU. This page is a public summary of how we handle support and KYC data.

Grievance & support

For questions, feedback, privacy requests, or grievances about the LuhaRide service:

Email: supportluharide@gmail.com

We aim to acknowledge genuine queries within a reasonable time and route serious safety or abuse reports appropriately.

KYC & verification documents

  • Purpose: Driver and union identity / vehicle documents are collected only to verify accounts, meet legal and safety expectations, and reduce fraud.
  • Retention: We keep these files only as long as needed for active verification, ongoing compliance, and dispute prevention while your account exists.
  • After account closure: We delete or anonymise verification documents within a reasonable period, except where applicable law requires longer retention.
  • Security: Files are stored on access-controlled servers with industry-standard safeguards. We do not sell document images or personal data to third parties for marketing.

User trust

We treat rider and driver data with care proportional to a transport marketplace: limited collection, secured storage, and the email above if something looks wrong.
