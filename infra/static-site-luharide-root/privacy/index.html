Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 April 2026 · Operated by TECHMCU

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how LuhaRide (operated by TECHMCU) collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application and website ("Service"). By using LuhaRide, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

LuhaRide is a taxi and ride-sharing platform connecting passengers with drivers and taxi unions in Uttarakhand and nearby areas. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile photo
  • Contact Details: WhatsApp number (optional, for driver-passenger communication)
  • Location Data: Your device location when you search for rides, create trips, or use map features (with your permission)
  • Verification Documents: For drivers and union admins - Aadhaar card, driving licence, vehicle RC (registration certificate), union registration documents, and related photos
  • Vehicle Information: Registration number, vehicle type, seating capacity, make and model
  • Trip and Booking Data: Routes, departure times, seat selections, fare amounts, booking status
  • Reviews and Ratings: Ratings and reviews you give to drivers, and ratings you receive from passengers

Usage Data:

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • IP address, browser type and version
  • App usage patterns, pages visited, features used
  • Login history, session information
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Provide the Service: Connect passengers with drivers, manage bookings, coordinate rides
  • Identity Verification: Verify driver and union admin identities using submitted documents (Aadhaar, DL, RC) to maintain platform safety and reduce fraud
  • Account Management: Create and maintain your account, authenticate logins, manage roles (passenger/driver/union admin)
  • Communication: Send notifications about bookings, trip updates, verification status, and service announcements
  • Safety and Security: Monitor for fraud, abuse, or safety concerns; investigate reported incidents
  • Improve Service: Analyze usage patterns to improve app features, fix bugs, and enhance user experience
  • Customer Support: Respond to your queries, complaints, and support requests
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations, respond to lawful requests from authorities

Document Verification and Storage

Purpose: Driver and union verification documents (Aadhaar, DL, RC, union photos) are collected exclusively for identity verification, fraud prevention, and regulatory compliance.

Watermarking: Uploaded documents are watermarked with "LuhaRide - Verification Copy" to prevent misuse.

Storage: Documents are stored on secure, access-controlled servers with encryption. Only authorized admin personnel can view these documents for verification purposes.

Retention: We retain verification documents only as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter as required for legal compliance, dispute resolution, and fraud prevention (typically up to 90 days after account deletion, unless law requires longer).

No Third-Party Sale: We do not sell, rent, or share your verification documents or personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Other Users: When you book a ride or create a trip, relevant information (name, photo, contact details for confirmed bookings) is shared between passenger and driver to facilitate the ride.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services (hosting providers, email services, SMS providers) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect rights, property, or safety.
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the new entity (you will be notified).
  • With Your Consent: We may share data for other purposes with your explicit consent.

We DO NOT sell your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

Location Services

LuhaRide uses your device location (with your permission) to:

  • Show nearby rides and destinations
  • Auto-fill pickup and drop-off locations
  • Display map-based features

You can enable or disable location access anytime through your device settings. Disabling location may limit certain features.

WhatsApp and Contact Sharing

LuhaRide may display driver's WhatsApp number and phone number to passengers after booking confirmation. This facilitates direct communication for trip coordination. We recommend using WhatsApp for booking-related queries only.

Important: Contact sharing is limited to confirmed bookings. Be cautious about sharing personal information outside the app.

Reviews and Ratings

Reviews and ratings you submit are visible to other users. They help maintain platform quality and user trust.

  • Reviews you write may be edited or removed if they violate our community guidelines
  • Ratings received by you from other users are preserved for platform integrity, even if you delete your account
  • You cannot delete ratings given to you by other users

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Essential Cookies: Maintain your login session, remember preferences
  • Analytics: Understand how you use the app, which features are popular
  • Performance: Monitor app performance, identify and fix bugs

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect some app features.

Account Deletion and Your Rights

You have the right to delete your account at any time.

How to Delete: Go to Profile → Settings → Delete account. You will be asked to confirm with your password.

What Gets Deleted:

  • Your profile and personal information (name, email, phone, photo)
  • Trips you created as a driver
  • Your bookings as a passenger
  • Reviews you submitted
  • Verification documents you uploaded (Aadhaar, DL, RC)
  • Your login history and notifications

What is Preserved:

  • Ratings given to you by other users (for platform data integrity)
  • Anonymized transaction records (for legal compliance and analytics)

Other Rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update incorrect information via Edit Profile
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, email us at supportluharide@gmail.com

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Verification Documents: Retained while your account is active and up to 90 days after deletion (unless law requires longer) for legal compliance and fraud prevention
  • Trip Records: Historical trip and booking data may be retained in anonymized form for analytics and legal compliance
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law (e.g., tax records, legal disputes)

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Password hashing (bcrypt) for account security
  • Access-controlled servers with restricted admin access
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Watermarked documents to prevent misuse

Important: No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Use strong passwords and report suspicious activity immediately.

Payments and Financial Data

LuhaRide does not handle payments. We are a connection platform only.

  • Fare payment is settled directly between passenger and driver (cash, UPI, or other agreed methods)
  • We do not collect, store, or process payment card information
  • We do not collect any payment transaction data
  • Payment disputes must be resolved between users directly

This means we have no access to your bank account, card details, or UPI information.

Third-Party Services

LuhaRide integrates with third-party services that may collect information:

  • SMS/Email Providers: For OTP verification and notifications
  • WhatsApp: When you choose to contact a driver via WhatsApp, you'll leave our app
  • Map Services: For location and route display
  • Cloud Hosting: Our servers run on secure cloud infrastructure

These third parties have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them when you use external links or services.

Children's Privacy

LuhaRide is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at supportluharide@gmail.com, and we will take steps to delete that information.

Age Requirement: To create a driver or union account, you must be at least 18 years old and hold a valid driving licence as required by law.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via:

  • In-app notification
  • Email to your registered address
  • Updated "Last updated" date on this page

Continued use of the Service after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Your Consent

By using LuhaRide, you consent to:

  • Collection and use of information as described in this policy
  • Processing of your data on servers located in India
  • Sharing of relevant information with other users (drivers/passengers) to facilitate rides

LuhaRide एक टैक्सी और राइड-शेयरिंग प्लेटफ़ॉर्म है जो उत्तराखंड और आसपास के क्षेत्रों में यात्रियों को ड्राइवरों और टैक्सी यूनियनों से जोड़ता है। हम आपकी गोपनीयता की रक्षा और डेटा को ज़िम्मेदारी से संभालने के लिए प्रतिबद्ध हैं।

हम क्या जानकारी एकत्र करते हैं

व्यक्तिगत जानकारी:

  • खाता जानकारी: नाम, ईमेल पता, फ़ोन नंबर, प्रोफ़ाइल फोटो
  • संपर्क विवरण: WhatsApp नंबर (वैकल्पिक, ड्राइवर-यात्री संचार के लिए)
  • स्थान डेटा: जब आप राइड खोजते हैं, यात्रा बनाते हैं या मैप सुविधाएँ उपयोग करते हैं तो आपके डिवाइस की स्थिति (आपकी अनुमति से)
  • सत्यापन दस्तावेज़: ड्राइवर और यूनियन एडमिन के लिए - आधार कार्ड, ड्राइविंग लाइसेंस, वाहन आरसी (पंजीकरण प्रमाण पत्र), यूनियन पंजीकरण दस्तावेज़ और संबंधित फ़ोटो
  • वाहन जानकारी: पंजीकरण संख्या, वाहन प्रकार, बैठने की क्षमता, मेक और मॉडल
  • यात्रा और बुकिंग डेटा: मार्ग, प्रस्थान समय, सीट चयन, किराया राशि, बुकिंग स्थिति
  • समीक्षा और रेटिंग: आप ड्राइवरों को दी गई रेटिंग और समीक्षाएँ, और यात्रियों से प्राप्त रेटिंग

उपयोग डेटा:

  • डिवाइस जानकारी (डिवाइस प्रकार, ऑपरेटिंग सिस्टम, विशिष्ट पहचानकर्ता)
  • IP पता, ब्राउज़र प्रकार और संस्करण
  • ऐप उपयोग पैटर्न, देखे गए पृष्ठ, उपयोग की गई सुविधाएँ
  • लॉगिन इतिहास, सत्र जानकारी
  • क्रैश रिपोर्ट और डायग्नोस्टिक डेटा

हम आपकी जानकारी का उपयोग कैसे करते हैं

हम आपके व्यक्तिगत डेटा का उपयोग निम्नलिखित उद्देश्यों के लिए करते हैं:

  • सेवा प्रदान करना: यात्रियों को ड्राइवरों से जोड़ना, बुकिंग प्रबंधित करना, राइड समन्वय
  • पहचान सत्यापन: प्लेटफ़ॉर्म सुरक्षा बनाए रखने और धोखाधड़ी कम करने के लिए जमा दस्तावेज़ों (आधार, डीएल, आरसी) से ड्राइवर और यूनियन एडमिन की पहचान सत्यापित करना
  • खाता प्रबंधन: आपका खाता बनाना और बनाए रखना, लॉगिन प्रमाणित करना, भूमिकाएँ प्रबंधित करना (यात्री/ड्राइवर/यूनियन एडमिन)
  • संचार: बुकिंग, यात्रा अपडेट, सत्यापन स्थिति और सेवा घोषणाओं की सूचनाएँ भेजना
  • सुरक्षा: धोखाधड़ी, दुरुपयोग या सुरक्षा चिंताओं की निगरानी; रिपोर्ट की गई घटनाओं की जाँच
  • सेवा सुधार: ऐप सुविधाएँ सुधारने, बग ठीक करने और उपयोगकर्ता अनुभव बढ़ाने के लिए उपयोग पैटर्न का विश्लेषण
  • ग्राहक सहायता: आपके प्रश्नों, शिकायतों और सहायता अनुरोधों का जवाब देना
  • कानूनी अनुपालन: कानूनी दायित्वों को पूरा करना, अधिकारियों के वैध अनुरोधों का जवाब देना

दस्तावेज़ सत्यापन और संग्रहण

उद्देश्य: ड्राइवर और यूनियन सत्यापन दस्तावेज़ (आधार, डीएल, आरसी, यूनियन फ़ोटो) विशेष रूप से पहचान सत्यापन, धोखाधड़ी रोकथाम और नियामक अनुपालन के लिए एकत्र किए जाते हैं।

वॉटरमार्किंग: अपलोड किए गए दस्तावेज़ों पर "LuhaRide - Verification Copy" वॉटरमार्क लगाया जाता है ताकि दुरुपयोग न हो।

संग्रहण: दस्तावेज़ सुरक्षित, एक्सेस-नियंत्रित सर्वर पर एन्क्रिप्शन के साथ संग्रहीत हैं। केवल अधिकृत एडमिन कर्मचारी ही सत्यापन के लिए ये दस्तावेज़ देख सकते हैं।

अवधारण: हम सत्यापन दस्तावेज़ तभी तक रखते हैं जब तक आपका खाता सक्रिय है और उसके बाद कानूनी अनुपालन, विवाद समाधान और धोखाधड़ी रोकथाम के लिए आवश्यक उचित अवधि तक (आमतौर पर खाता हटाने के 90 दिन बाद तक, जब तक कानून अधिक समय की आवश्यकता न हो)।

तृतीय-पक्ष बिक्री नहीं: हम आपके सत्यापन दस्तावेज़ या व्यक्तिगत डेटा को विपणन उद्देश्यों के लिए तृतीय पक्षों को बेचते, किराए पर देते या साझा नहीं करते।

डेटा साझाकरण और प्रकटीकरण

हम आपकी जानकारी निम्नलिखित स्थितियों में साझा कर सकते हैं:

  • अन्य उपयोगकर्ताओं के साथ: जब आप राइड बुक करते हैं या यात्रा बनाते हैं, तो राइड सुविधा के लिए यात्री और ड्राइवर के बीच संबंधित जानकारी (नाम, फोटो, पुष्टि बुकिंग के लिए संपर्क विवरण) साझा की जाती है।
  • सेवा प्रदाता: हम तृतीय-पक्ष सेवाओं (होस्टिंग, ईमेल, SMS प्रदाता) का उपयोग करते हैं जो हमारी ओर से सख्त गोपनीयता समझौतों के तहत डेटा संसाधित करते हैं।
